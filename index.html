The itinerary

6 days, city by city.

2 countries, 3 cities, zero fuss. Amsterdam to Bruges to Paris, the most cinematic route in Western Europe, done properly.

Day 01
Arrive in Amsterdam

Land at Schiphol, transfer to the hotel, and take the afternoon at your own pace. Amsterdam doesn't need to be rushed, but even a first evening walk along the canals will tell you exactly why you chose to come here. The tour briefing is at hotel reception. Tomorrow, we go properly.

Arrival Hotel check in Free evening
Day 02
Amsterdam city tour, Zaanse Schans & Volendam

The full Amsterdam experience: Zaanse Schans with its iconic windmills and wooden houses, the fishing village of Volendam on the Markermeer, and a diamond demo in the city. End the day with a transfer to the lively city centre where dinner choices are genuinely excellent. This is the day your camera roll starts filling up fast.

Zaanse Schans Volendam Diamond demo City centre
Day 03
Bruges & Ghent day trip, arrive in Paris

Full-day through medieval Belgium before Paris. Ghent's Castle of the Counts and St. Bavo's Cathedral, then Bruges the "Venice of the North" with its cobblestone Market Square and the Basilica of the Holy Blood. Belgian chocolate is mandatory. Then we drive to Paris. You arrive, step out onto a Parisian street, and immediately get it.

Bruges Ghent Belgian chocolate Paris arrival
Day 04
Versailles, Montmartre & Paris city tour

The Palace of Versailles in the morning, genuinely one of the most over-the-top beautiful things you'll ever walk through. Then Montmartre by cable car, the artists' quarter, the Sacré-Cœur at the top of the hill, and the city views that make Paris feel like it was designed to be looked at from above. Optional: an evening show. Worth considering.

Versailles Montmartre Cable car Sacré-Cœur
Day 05
Eiffel Tower & Seine cruise

Up to the second floor of the Eiffel Tower tickets included, views life-changing. Then the Seine cruise, drifting past Notre-Dame, the Louvre, Pont Neuf. The afternoon is yours: the Champs-Élysées for shopping, a café for sitting, or just more wandering. Paris rewards a slow afternoon. The last evening as a group Twinkle's pick for dinner. Trust the recommendation.

Eiffel Tower Seine cruise Free afternoon Group dinner
Day 06
Fly home from Paris

Last breakfast in Paris. Check out. Airport. You board with a very full camera roll, an unfair amount of French pastry knowledge, and the kind of trip that gets brought up in conversation for years. Six days was enough to fall in love. Next time, longer.

Departure Last breakfast
